A hauntingly powerful portrayal of one man's transformation from opportunistic businessman to humanitarian, as he risks everything to save over a thousand Jews during the Holocaust.

Schindler's List is a deeply moving historical drama set during World War II, following the true story of Oskar Schindler, a German businessman who profits from the war by employing Jewish workers in his factories. Initially driven by personal gain, Schindler gradually becomes aware of the horrors of the Holocaust and the fate of the Jewish community under Nazi rule. As the atrocities intensify, Schindlerâ€™s moral compass shifts, leading him to make life-altering decisions that impact the lives of those around him.

The Silence of the Lambs

A riveting psychological thriller that follows an FBI trainee's chilling pursuit of a serial killer, with the help of an imprisoned, manipulative cannibalistic psychiatrist.

The Silence of the Lambs is a chilling psychological thriller that follows Clarice Starling, a young FBI trainee tasked with interviewing Dr. Hannibal Lecter, a brilliant but imprisoned cannibalistic psychiatrist, to gain insights into the mind of a serial killer known as Buffalo Bill. As Clarice delves deeper into the investigation, she must navigate the complex and manipulative relationship with Lecter, who offers cryptic clues while probing into her past and fears.
The film explores themes of power, control, and the nature of evil, as Clarice races against time to prevent further murders. With its haunting atmosphere and unforgettable performances, particularly by Anthony Hopkins as Lecter, The Silence of the Lambs masterfully blends elements of horror and suspense, creating a tense narrative that leaves a lasting impact on its audience. Directed by Jonathan Demme, this iconic film remains a landmark in the thriller genre.

Titanic

An unforgettable tale of love, sacrifice, and fate, set against the backdrop of one of history's greatest maritime disasters.

Titanic is a sweeping romantic drama that follows the ill-fated maiden voyage of the RMS Titanic in 1912. The film centers on Jack Dawson, a penniless artist, and Rose DeWitt Bukater, a young woman from a wealthy family, who meet aboard the luxurious ship. Despite their different social backgrounds, they form a deep and passionate bond that challenges societal norms and expectations. As their love blossoms, Rose grapples with her impending arranged marriage and the constraints of her upper-class life.
The film beautifully captures the opulence of the Titanic and the emotional struggles of its characters, all while highlighting themes of love, sacrifice, and resilience. As the voyage takes a disastrous turn, Jack and Rose must navigate the chaos around them, fighting to survive against overwhelming odds. Directed by James Cameron, Titanic is a poignant tribute to love enduring in the face of tragedy, leaving a lasting impact on audiences worldwide.
